How to Donate

Online

Donate

  1. Click the DONATE button or visit: https://support.afi.com/thesisfilms1617

  2. Select “1705-AND WE FELL” in the dropdown menu

  3. Fill out your information & donation amount *

    • * Please note, all donations are in USD

  4. Click "Donate Now"

By Mail

  1. Make check payable to "American Film Institute"

  2. Note "And We Fell #1705" in the check memo

  3. Mail to:
    Thesis #1705, And We Fell
    C/O American Film Institute
    2021 N. Western Ave.
    Los Angeles, CA 90027

In-Kind Donations

Help comes in many forms. We will gratefully accept any in-kind donations to our film - and you will make the credits! Please contact us and let us know how you can help.
We'll gladly accept any of following:

  1. Filming Locations (Houses, School Gyms)
  2. Equipment (Camera, Grip, Lighting)
  3. Art Department & Construction Supplies
  4. Visual Effects
  5. Color Correction
  6. Sound Mixing
  7. Music Composing
  8. Craft Services/Catering
  9. Wrestling Uniforms/Gear
  10. Stunt Services

Matching Grants

Many companies will match donations made by their employees to non-profit charitable institutions. This can DOUBLE your donation. Your Human Resources Department will be able to inform you if your employer offers this benefit. If so, please select the “My company will match my gift” on the AFI Web Donation Portal. Please contact us for additional help.

Tax Deductions

The American Film Institute is a Government recognized 501(c)(3) Non-Profit Organization, and holds tax exempt status. Donations made toward “And We Fell” may be tax deductible under Section 170 of the U.S. Tax Code. A letter of acknowledgement from AFI will be sent for all contributions.
